List of Datasets

      Here is a collection of interesting datasets that are great for practicing data analysis and visualizations! These datasets were created by other users and were found on sites including Data World, Reddit, Kaggle, Serebii, and many more. Consequently, these datasets were not created by me and credit should be given to the users who went and webscraped the data. Below, you will find a table that includes the information in the Dublin Core format. The last time that this data table was updated was on July 25, 2020. If you find another dataset that you think should be included in this archive of Pokémon datasets, please contact me here!
